Accessibility Overview

The chart compares the employment for disabled and non-disabled Americans in 1991 and 2010, respectively.
The bar on the left each year represents the employment rate for disabled Americans.
The bar on the right represents that for the non-disabled.
It is worth noticing that the bar on the right is always higher than the bar on the left.
The employment rate for disabled Americans is 50.2% in 1991, and 41.1% in 2010.
The employment rate for non-disabled Americans is 84.4% in 1991, and 79.1% in 2010.
